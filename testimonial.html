    From my Form Teachers

    Lim Qiao Zhen was a student of Tampines Secondary School from 2020 to 2023.
    Throughout her academic journey, Qiao Zhen has consistently demonstrated a bright
    and diligent attitude towards her studies. She is highly motivated and determined to
    excel. She has clear goals for herself and works towards them with determination.
    Qiao Zhen is receptive to advice and will ask questions in class whenever in doubt.

    She shows a particularly strong affinity for Mathematics and Sciences and performed
    well for both subjects. She is aware of her strengths and weaknesses and works hard
    towards improving her results, thus allowing her to obtain top 25% in the cohort in
    2020 and 2021.

    Qiao Zhen is a reliable team player who is equally adept at working independently.
    Her ability to communicate effectively has allowed her to engage with her peers and
    earn their respect. Her strong work ethic and determination have always been an
    inspiration to those around her. In group settings, Qiao Zhen is always willing to lend
    a helping hand and take the initiative to get things done. At the same time, her
    independent work has been characterized by a meticulous attention to detail and a
    commitment to excellence.

    Qiao Zhen was a dedicated and committed member of the National Police Cadet
    Corps (NPCC) from 2020 to 2023. Throughout her time in NPCC, she proved to be
    an outstanding member who consistently showed up to training sessions with an
    unwavering determination to improve. It is no surprise that she was appointed as the
    Chairperson of NPCC, a testament to her leadership skills and commitment to the
    organization. In her role, she demonstrated excellent communication skills and a
    democratic leadership style, valuing the opinions of others while still maintaining a
    clear direction for the team. She fulfilled her duties with a strong sense of responsibility
    and displayed great initiative, always going above and beyond what was expected of
    her.

    Besides her commitments to her CCA and studies, Qiao Zhen is also sensitive to the
    needs of the people in the wider community as can be seen from her active
    participation in the Values in Action activities. In 2021, she and her classmates
    created a video to spread awareness about anti-cyberbullying. Qiao Zhen is also
    concerned about animal welfare and participated in the class project on promoting
    SPCA in 2022 and spread awareness about the mistreatment and abuse of animals.
    She had also supported the fund raising for Caregiving Welfare Association (CWA) in
    2023 and helped to raise public awareness about CWA. She participated in the Flag
    Day to gather donations for those in need.

    Qiao Zhen’s passion for learning and self-improvement is admirable and she
    constantly seeks out opportunities to broaden her knowledge and skills. She will
    continue to achieve great things in her academic and personal pursuits. Qiao Zhen’s
    ability to handle challenges and her strong work ethic make her a valuable asset to
    any team or organization.
